## Invited Talk — Texas Robotics Symposium 2026

**"A Framework to Evaluate the Agency of AI Agents"** | The University of Texas at Austin | **March 16, 2026**

Presented an evaluation framework for agentic AI systems, addressing a critical gap in the industry: inconsistent terminology and inflated claims around "AI agents." The framework assesses systems across six capability dimensions — Perception, Decisioning, Actioning, Agency, Adaptability, and Knowledge — drawing from Gartner research and original analysis of 150+ companies across 14 verticals.

**Key finding:** ~69% of products marketed as "AI agents" are closer to agentic assistants or workflow tools than fully autonomous agents. SRE emerged as the most mature category (avg. score 3.15).

## Best Research Paper Award — IIT Indore

**"Attention-guided Generator with Dual Discriminator GAN for Real-time Video Anomaly Detection"** | **February 24, 2025**

Awarded Best Research Paper in Computer Science & Engineering at Indian Institute of Technology Indore. The paper introduces A2D-GAN — a novel framework integrating multi-stage self-attention with dual discriminators for improved real-time video anomaly detection. Published in _Engineering Applications of Artificial Intelligence_ (Volume 131, 2024).

**Collaborators:** Anikeit Sethi, Rituraj Singh | **Supervisors:** Prof. Aruna Tiwari, Dr. Sanjay Singh
